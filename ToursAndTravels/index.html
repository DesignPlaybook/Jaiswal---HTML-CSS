Upcoming Event - Prayagraj

Embark on an unforgettable journey to the spiritual heart of India with our upcoming trip to Prayagraj! Renowned for its cultural richness and divine significance, Prayagraj is home to the iconic Triveni Sangam, the confluence of the Ganga, Yamuna, and the mystical Saraswati rivers. This thoughtfully curated trip offers an opportunity to explore the city's historic landmarks, including the majestic Allahabad Fort, Anand Bhavan, and serene ghats. Witness the timeless rituals performed at the Sangam and soak in the vibrant local culture. Whether you’re a history enthusiast, a spiritual seeker, or simply looking to experience the beauty of India’s heritage, this trip promises a truly enriching experience. Join us for this soulful adventure and create memories to cherish forever!
